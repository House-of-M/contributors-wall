House of M

Contributors Wall.

An initiative by the House of M to help open source project contributors get a quick shutout on a public wall! This small project is dedicated to those who contribute to open source public projects from or outside this organization.

Regarding Hacktoberfest

This project is also taking part in the Hacktoberfest event to introduce younglings and total bigginers to open source contribution by having them make a simple pull request to have their own contributor's card on this live website.

It's an easy task, good first issue for coders and non-coders alike. Veteran open source contributors are also welcome to tag their name on the wall and share their open source experience!

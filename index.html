Contributors Wall.

This project is meant to introduce younglings and total bigginers to open source contribution by having them make a simple pull request to have their own contributor's card on this live website.

It's an easy task, good first issue for coders and non-coders alike. The only skills you need are the power of Copy/Past and paying attention to instruction to understand the basic pipleine of open source contribution on GitHub.

Veteran open source contributors are also welcome to tag their name on the wall!
